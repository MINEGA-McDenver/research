FORMS OF RAPING


1. Stranger Rape: This occurs when the perpetrator is unknown to the victim.
2. Acquaintance Rape: Involves someone known to the victim, such as a friend or partner.
3. Marital Rape: Non-consensual sex between spouses.
4. Statutory Rape: Engaging in sexual activity with someone below the age of consent...
5. Gang Rape: Involves multiple perpetrators assaulting one victim.
